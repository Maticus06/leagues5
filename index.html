Combat Masteries

Combat Masteries offer specialized boosts to combat styles. Players can unlock up to 10 mastery points by completing specific tasks. Each mastery tier builds upon the previous one in its respective combat style. A unique passive bonus is unlocked upon reaching the first mastery in any style.

Unlocking Combat Masteries

  • Defeat a Giant.
  • Defeat 10 monsters with a combat level of 100+.
  • Defeat Scurrius by yourself.
  • Defeat a monster with a Slayer requirement of 55+.
  • Defeat TzTok-Jad in the Fight Caves.
  • Reach a combat level of 100.
  • Defeat an Echo Boss.
  • Defeat two unique Echo Bosses.
  • Defeat three unique Echo Bosses.
  • Defeat TzKal-Zuk.

Passive Buffs

Unlocking the first tier of any combat mastery grants a passive buff to all combat styles:

  • +5% accuracy and damage for all combat styles.
  • +5% hitpoints regeneration rate.
  • These bonuses stack with other relic and mastery effects.

Regions

You can unlock up to three additional regions beyond the starting areas of Misthalin and Karamja. Each region offers unique benefits and activities. Choose based on your preferred playstyle and goals.

Asgarnia

  • Pros:

    • Access to Cerberus, an Echo Boss offering powerful rewards.
    • Home to the Motherlode Mine, perfect for Mining-related relics like Power Miner.
    • Includes the Warriors' Guild for defenders and combat training.
    • Provides access to Pest Control for Void Knight gear with boosted minigame rewards.

    Cons:

    • Less focus on high-end Magic gear or bosses.
    • Requires Slayer levels for some content like Cerberus.

Fremennik Isles

  • Pros:

    • Access to Dagannoth Kings, an Echo Boss offering versatile combat challenges and valuable drops.
    • Provides unique items like Fremennik helms and rings.
    • Includes Rellekka Slayer Dungeon for a variety of tasks.

    Cons:

    • Limited skilling opportunities compared to other regions.
    • Focuses heavily on combat, which may not suit all players.

Kandarin

  • Pros:

    • Thermonuclear Smoke Devil Echo Boss for a mix of combat and utility.
    • Access to Fishing Guild, Baxtorian Falls, and Seers' Village for skilling.
    • Includes the Barbarian Assault minigame with boosted rewards.
    • Home to Zulrah, an efficient and manageable Magic-based boss.

    Cons:

    • Skilling content may overlap with other regions.

Morytania

  • Pros:

    • Home to Grotesque Guardians, a unique Echo Boss.
    • Includes Barrows, offering Magic-based combat rewards.
    • Hallowed Sepulchre for rewarding agility training.
    • Strong Slayer opportunities with monsters like Aberrant Spectres and Nechryael.

    Cons:

    • Requires Slayer levels for some content.
    • Heavily combat-focused, with limited skilling opportunities.

Kourend and Kebos

  • Pros:

    • Hespori Echo Boss for Nature-based rewards.
    • Access to Farming Guild and Woodcutting Guild for skilling.
    • Home to Chambers of Xeric with boosted rewards.
    • Variety of Slayer tasks in Catacombs of Kourend.

    Cons:

    • Echo Boss and Chambers of Xeric may require advanced setups.

Desert

  • Pros:

    • Access to Kalphite Queen Echo Boss with valuable drops.
    • Includes Tombs of Amascut for powerful rewards.
    • Home to Desert Treasure quests and Ancient Magicks.
    • Boosted rewards from Guardians of the Rift.

    Cons:

    • High-level combat challenges may not suit all players.
    • Limited skilling content compared to other regions.

Wilderness

  • Pros:

    • Home to King Black Dragon Echo Boss.
    • Access to Revenants for profitable drops.
    • Includes Wilderness Agility Course for training.
    • Variety of skilling and combat activities in high-risk areas.

    Cons:

    • High risk due to PvP mechanics.
    • Requires caution and preparation for safe participation.

Tirannwn

  • Pros:

    • Access to the Gauntlet and Crystal Weaponry.
    • Unique skilling content like crystal trees.
    • Includes Tirannwn agility course.

    Cons:

    • Content may require advanced setups.
    • Limited to specific combat and skilling activities.

Varlamore

  • Pros:

    • Home to Sol Heredit, a Grandmaster Echo Boss.
    • Access to Mastering Mixology for advanced potion-making.
    • Variety of new skilling activities.

    Cons:

    • Some content may require high levels or advanced gear.
    • Less known strategies due to being a new region.
